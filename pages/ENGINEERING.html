Nurse Susan / Capstone Design Project

Nurse Susan was a project to create a design that would assist an elderly wheel-chair user in transfering from wheelchair to bed, and vice versa. The design included two subsystems; an inflating lift system for the chair, and rotational/support system to allow the user to turn around safely to sit. The team utilized a formal design process, including competitive analysis, customer requirements, engineering characteristics, conceptual and embodiment design, prototyping and testing, and manufacturing cost analysis. The team was constrained to milestone deadlines as well as a budget, and worked in increments to deliver a final working prototype.

Gorilla.Alpha / Bio-Inspired Robotics Design

CAD Assembly of Gorilla Robot

Gorilla.Alpha is a bio-inspired robot that mimics the knuckle-walking gait of a silverback gorilla. All unique components were designed in Solidworks CAD Software, and then 3D-printed for the structure. A dynamic analysis was used to define the gait, which was then translated into Arduino code. The code was sent to a microcontroller which would translate the dynamics of the gait into corresponding rotation of servo motors, controlling the segments of the legs. The final design was able to walk (like a gorilla!) for over 30x its body length on both carpet and asphalt.
